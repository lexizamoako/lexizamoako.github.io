Dr Alex Owusu Amoakoh

LJMU Natural Capital Hub · Lead Spatial Analyst

Profile

I am the Lead Spatial Analyst at Liverpool John Moores University’s Natural Capital Hub. My work spans research, tool‑building, and policy support for nature recovery and climate resilience. I collaborate with public, private, and NGO partners across the UK and internationally.
